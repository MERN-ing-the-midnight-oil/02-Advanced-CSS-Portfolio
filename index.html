About Me

I'm a K-12 science eduator and an educational games and app creator. Citizen Giants

Citizen Giants is where school aged students and adult citizen scientists can stand on the shoulders of giants. As a k-12 science teacher I've taught the importance of publication and replication in science. As a science fair judge and mentor I have seen countless original ideas meet premature deaths at the ends of school years. Science fair projects are rarely built upon or replicated in subsequent years in a way that reflects how science should actually work. Existing platforms for publishing elementary, middle, and high school science fair projects are few or regional and the ones that do exist are not accessable for young authors. Citizen Giants is a place for all learners to contribute to and build upon each-other's scientific understanding, publish their observations about the natural world, offer reviews and critiques, and replicate other's findings.
